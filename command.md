## Strict Categories (Grouped by Specific Systems)

### Hunting System
Commands related to hunting activities, stats, and equipment.
- `hunt` (Go on hunting expeditions)
- `hunting` (View hunting stats and profile)
- `huntshop` (Buy hunting equipment)

### Heist System
Commands for planning, joining, and executing heists.
- `heist` (View heist information)
- `planheist` (Plan a new heist)
- `joinheist` (Join an existing heist)
- `executeheist` (Execute a planned heist)

### Pet System
Commands for buying, managing, and caring for pets.
- `buypet` (Purchase a pet)
- `pets` (View your pets)
- `petcare` (Care for your pets)

### Family System
Commands for managing family members and trips.
- `family` (View family information)
- `addfamily` (Add family members)
- `trip` (Send family on trips)

### Business System
Commands for starting and managing businesses.
- `business` (View business information)
- `startbusiness` (Start a new business)

### Vehicle System
Commands for buying, managing, and racing cars.
- `buycar` (Purchase a car)
- `garage` (View your cars)
- `race` (Race your car)

### Housing System
Commands for buying and managing houses.
- `buyhouse` (Purchase a house)
- `myhome` (View your house)

### Role System
Commands for buying and viewing roles.
- `buyrole` (Purchase a role)
- `myroles` (View your roles)

### Gambling System
Commands for gambling and risky bets.
- `gamble` (Bet on games)
- `rob` (Attempt to rob another player)

### Shopping System
Commands for buying and selling items.
- `shop` (Browse the shop)
- `sell` (Sell items)

### Currency Management System
Commands for managing currency, deposits, and transfers.
- `balance` (Check your balance)
- `deposit` (Deposit money)
- `withdraw` (Withdraw money)
- `donate` (Donate money to others)
- `transactionhistory` (View transaction history)
- `vault` (Access vault)

### Earning System
Commands for earning money through various activities.
- `daily` (Claim daily reward)
- `weekly` (Claim weekly reward)
- `beg` (Beg for money)
- `work` (Work to earn money)

### Profile and Leaderboards System
Commands for viewing profiles and leaderboards.
- `profile` (View your profile)
- `leaderboard` (View leaderboards)

### Inventory System
Commands for managing inventory.
- `inventory` (View your inventory)

### Upgrade System
Commands for upgrading items or properties.
- `upgrade` (Upgrade items)

### General Economy System
General economy information and help.
- `economy` (General economy info)
